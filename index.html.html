Our Story

Decades of flavor.
Family at the heart.

Szechuan House has been a beloved fixture in Baltimore's dining scene for decades. Run by Robert, Jean, and the whole family, our kitchen honors the bold traditions of Szechuan cooking — fiery, fragrant, and deeply satisfying. Now reopened at Timonium Square, we're back where we belong: feeding our neighbors.
